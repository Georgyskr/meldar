## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**Overall plan score: 3.2/10 as written. 7.5/10 with the fixes below.**

The original plan correctly identified the MedVi playbook's power — aggressive multi-channel paid acquisition with manufactured authority. But it copied the *tactics* (persona farms, fake social proof, volume campaigns) without adapting to Meldar's *reality* (zero users, zero data, solo founder, $20K budget, career SaaS not pain-driven medical purchase).

**The plan as written will fail within 4-6 weeks** due to: platform bans (persona farm), legal exposure (fake testimonials), budget waste (250 campaigns on $5K), and execution collapse (solo founder can't run 6 channels + 500 SEO pages + enterprise sales simultaneously).

**The revised plan below preserves MedVi's strategic principles** while adapting execution to reality.

## UNANIMOUS FINDINGS (flagged by 3+ agents)

### 1. KILL THE FAKE SOCIAL PROOF (flagged by 8/13 agents)
**Agents:** Auditor, Creative, Paid Social, Social Media, Growth Hacker, LinkedIn, SEO, AI Citation

- Fake "As seen in" logos → FTC fines up to $50K/violation, instant ad disapproval on Meta
- AI-generated fake testimonials → violates FTC Endorsement Guides, EU AI Act, UK Online Safety Act
- Fake case studies → disqualifies enterprise sales immediately
- A product that audits digital presence using fake social proof is a **brand-destroying contradiction**
- AI citation systems (Claude, ChatGPT) detect fabricated proof and **deprioritize** those sites

**FIX:** Real (even modest) social proof from Day 1. Prompt every free scan user for review. Offer first 50 users lifetime discount for testimonial rights. Use real scan data as proof ("10,000+ footprints analyzed").

### 2. PERSONA FARM AT SCALE = GUARANTEED BAN (flagged by 6/13 agents)
**Agents:** PPC, Auditor, Paid Social, Social Media, LinkedIn, Creative

- 50-100 Facebook pages triggers Coordinated Inauthentic Behavior detection → 90%+ ban within 60 days
- Loses ALL ad accounts, pixel data, and audience data when banned
- LinkedIn aggressively detects fakes (phone/ID verification, IP fingerprinting) → 30-40% attrition in 90 days
- Even MedVi got caught — they only survived because they were already at $400M revenue

**FIX:** 5-10 carefully managed persona pages (staggered creation, separate Business Managers, differentiated content) + founder-led personal brand + 20-50 real ambassadors/advocates.

### 3. BUDGET/CAMPAIGN VOLUME MISMATCH (flagged by 5/13 agents)
**Agents:** PPC, Creative, Paid Social, Auditor, Programmatic

- 250 concurrent campaigns on $5K/mo = $0.67/day per campaign → none exit learning phase
- Meta needs ~50 conversions/week per ad set to optimize
- Kill trigger at 500 impressions is statistically meaningless (need 30+ conversions)
- $5K→$50K in 12 weeks violates Meta's 20%/week budget increase rule

**FIX:** Start with 4-6 consolidated campaigns across 3-5 pages. $250-500 minimum per campaign/month. Scale 20%/week max. Realistic path to $50K/mo: 5-6 months, not 12 weeks.

### 4. VALIDATE BEFORE SCALING (flagged by 4/13 agents)
**Agents:** Auditor, PPC, Sales Engineer, Growth Hacker

- Spending $20K+ across 6 channels with 0 signups and 0 conversion proof
- Need 50-100 real conversions before building a scaling plan
- Enterprise sales premature at Week 8 — need B2C proof first

**FIX:** $2-3K validation sprint on Meta + Google only. Prove CPA, conversion rate, and retention before adding channels. Enterprise deferred to Week 16-20.

### 5. SOLO FOUNDER EXECUTION CEILING (flagged by 4/13 agents)
**Agents:** Auditor, Social Media, LinkedIn, Video

- 250 campaigns + 6 channels + 20 creatives/week + 500 SEO pages + enterprise sales = impossible
- Realistic ceiling: 50-70 content pieces/week with AI assistance
- 10-12 hrs/week for video is achievable; 40+ hrs/week for everything is not

**FIX:** Ruthlessly prioritize. 2 paid channels (Meta + Google). 2 organic channels (LinkedIn + YouTube). Everything else is Phase 2 after proving unit economics.

## HIGHEST-VALUE ADDITIONS (things missing from the original plan)

### From Growth Hacker:
- **Instant scan with shareable scorecard** — ditch the 5-min quiz for value-first: name input → results in 60 seconds → shareable social card. K-factor target: 0.3-0.5.
- **Referral from Day 1, not Week 6** — every user without share mechanic = wasted viral opportunity
- **North Star metric: Weekly Scans Completed** (captures acquisition, activation, virality, retention in one number)
- **Self-reinforcing flywheel** — each scan feeds data that improves the product, which drives more scans

### From SEO Specialist:
- **Kill 500 programmatic pages, build 10-20 free tools instead** — "Google Yourself" tool (10K-20K monthly searches), LinkedIn Headline Generator, Resume Keyword Checker. Tools satisfy intent, attract links, convert directly.
- **HARO/Connectively is dead** (shut down 2024) — use Terkel, Qwoted, podcast appearances

### From Search Query Analyst:
- **"Google yourself" keyword cluster is missing** — 40K-60K monthly searches, maps perfectly to product
- **6 additional keyword clusters totaling 140K-210K monthly searches** identified
- **Day-1 negative keyword list** prevents 25-40% budget waste

### From Video Specialist:
- **Screen-share format, NOT AI avatars** — more authentic, faster to produce, avoids uncanny valley
- **One-to-many content multiplication** — 1 video → 10-12 assets across platforms
- **YouTube as primary organic channel** alongside LinkedIn (inherently visual product)

### From AI Citation Strategist:
- **Allow AI crawlers in robots.txt** — zero cost, high impact (GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot)
- **Publish original research** ("Digital Footprint Statistics 2026") — AI systems disproportionately cite unique statistics
- **No tool owns "career-specific digital footprint scan"** — this intersection is open
- **Custom GPT** for ChatGPT ecosystem presence

### From Paid Social Strategist:
- **Conversions API is non-negotiable** before any Meta spend — 40-60% data blindness without it
- **Hub-and-spoke model**: 5-8 campaigns per ad account, CBO prospecting, ABO retargeting, Advantage+ for 30-40%

### From Social Media Strategist:
- **Reddit and Quora** are completely missing — prime channels for career/AI anxiety topics
- **Facebook group infiltration** — join existing 50K+ career groups rather than building from scratch
- **LinkedIn polls get 3-5x reach** — missed entirely

### From LinkedIn Content Creator:
- **Realistic follower targets**: 300-500 in 30 days, 2K-4K in 90 days (not 10K)
- **No external links in post bodies** — LinkedIn suppresses outbound links, always use first comment
- **Engagement drives growth more than content volume** — prioritize commenting over posting
- **30-day content calendar and 20 hook templates** provided in review

### From Creative Strategist:
- **"Threat-Reveal-Rescue" framework** for all ad creative
- **Persona-specific creative**: Recruiter for cold (alarm), Coach for retargeting (nurture), Hiring Manager for B2B (authority)
- **8-week testing roadmap** with specific test sequence

### From Sales Engineer:
- **B2C-first, enterprise-later** — realistic first paid enterprise customer at Week 20-30
- **B2C pricing ($29/mo) is higher than B2B per-seat ($15-25)** — backwards, needs fixing
- **Free 10-person team pilot → case study rights → paid expansion** path

### From Programmatic Buyer:
- **Demandbase/6Sense is unrealistic** ($30-120K/yr) — use LinkedIn Matched Audiences for 80% of benefit at $0 incremental
- **Exclude all mobile app inventory from GDN** — saves 20-40% wasted display spend
- **CTV/OTT premature** — revisit at $50K+/mo total spend
