# MELDAR MARKETING BLITZ PLAN
## MedVi-Inspired Full-Internet Domination Strategy

**Product**: Meldar — Digital Footprint Scan + AI Career Tools
**Target ICP**: Professionals 28-45 worried about AI replacing them, corporate HR/L&D
**Markets**: EU, UK, USA, Ukraine
**Current State**: Landing page + quiz + xray live, 0 signups
**Goal**: Replicate MedVi's "every channel, maximum aggression" approach adapted for career/AI SaaS

---

## PHASE 0: INFRASTRUCTURE (Week 1-2)

### 0.1 AI Creative Pipeline
- Set up Midjourney workspace for professional headshots, lifestyle images, transformation visuals
- Set up Runway/HeyGen for video testimonial generation
- Set up ElevenLabs voices for voiceover content
- Build ChatGPT/Claude prompt library for ad copy at volume (50+ variants per campaign)
- Create "career transformation" before/after template (stressed → confident professional)

### 0.2 Persona Page Farm
- **Facebook**: Create 50-100 persona pages — career coaches, recruiters, hiring managers, HR insiders, tech leads
  - AI-generated headshots (Midjourney) — professional, trustworthy, diverse demographics
  - Each page: unique name, bio, professional backstory
  - Categorize as "Education" or "Personal Blog" (avoid "Business" for lighter moderation)
  - Seed each with 10-15 organic posts before running any ads
  - Budget $500-1,000/page for follower campaigns → 5K-10K followers each at ~$0.01/follow
- **LinkedIn**: Create 20-30 personal profiles as thought leader personas (riskier — LinkedIn verifies more)
  - Alternative: Use real Meldar team accounts + ghostwritten content
- **Twitter/X**: Create 30-50 persona accounts for career/AI commentary
- **TikTok**: 10-20 accounts posting AI career advice short-form content

### 0.3 Tracking & Attribution
- Meta Pixel + Conversions API on all Meldar properties
- Google Tag Manager with enhanced ecommerce
- UTM taxonomy: platform_persona_campaign_creative
- Multi-touch attribution model (first-touch for acquisition, last-touch for conversion)
- GA4 with custom events for quiz steps, scan start, scan complete, upgrade

### 0.4 Landing Page & Funnel
- **Primary landing page**: Bold hook → social proof bar → pain points → CTA
  - Hook options: "Your digital footprint is costing you jobs", "AI knows more about you than your resume says", "83% of recruiters Google you before calling"
  - Fake "As seen in" media bar (TechCrunch, Forbes, Wired logos)
  - AI-generated testimonial carousel with names + headshots
  - Single CTA: "Get Your Free Scan"
- **Quiz funnel** (3-5 minutes):
  - Step 1: Role, industry, years of experience
  - Step 2: Current job search status, career goals
  - Step 3: LinkedIn usage, online presence awareness
  - Step 4: Email capture
  - Step 5: Display "Digital Visibility Score: XX/100" (calculated but dramatic — always concerning enough to drive action)
  - Step 6: Show specific issues found → pricing for full scan + fix recommendations
- **Pricing presentation**: 
  - Anchor: "Companies pay $5,000+ for executive digital audits"
  - First month: $29 (loss leader)
  - Ongoing: $49/mo or $399/yr
  - Free tier: basic scan only (no recommendations, no monitoring)

## PHASE 1: PAID ACQUISITION — META/FACEBOOK (Week 2-6)

### 1.1 Campaign Architecture
- **Tier 1 — Brand**: Meldar branded campaigns (10% of budget)
- **Tier 2 — Problem-aware**: "AI replacing jobs", "digital footprint", "recruiters googling you" (40% of budget)
- **Tier 3 — Solution-aware**: "career AI tools", "LinkedIn optimization", "online reputation" (30% of budget)
- **Tier 4 — Competitor conquest**: alternatives to resume builders, LinkedIn premium, career coaching (20% of budget)

### 1.2 Persona Page Ad Deployment
- Each persona page runs 3-5 ad campaigns simultaneously
- 50 pages × 5 campaigns = 250 concurrent campaigns minimum
- Ad formats per page:
  - UGC-style "expert recommendation" video (AI-generated)
  - Carousel: "5 things recruiters find when they Google you"
  - Single image: transformation story ("How I went from invisible to 10 recruiter messages/week")
  - Reels: 15-30s career tips with hook
- Copy pattern: personal authority → problem identification → solution → CTA
  - "As a recruiter with 15 years of experience, I check every candidate's digital footprint. Here's what most people don't know..."

### 1.3 Targeting
- **Primary**: Professionals 28-45, interests: career development, job searching, LinkedIn, AI/technology, management
- **Secondary**: Recently changed jobs (Facebook life event targeting), job title targeting
- **Lookalikes**: 1%, 3%, 5% from quiz completers → scan purchasers
- **Retargeting**: Quiz abandoners (step-specific), landing page visitors, video viewers (25%, 50%, 75%)
- **Geo**: Start USA + UK (English), expand to EU + Ukraine with localized creative

### 1.4 Budget & Scaling
- Start: $5,000/month test budget across 10 pages
- Scale trigger: any campaign hitting <$5 CPA for quiz completion
- Scale path: $5K → $15K → $50K → $150K/month over 12 weeks
- Kill trigger: any campaign >$15 CPA after 500 impressions

### 1.5 Creative Production Volume
- Week 1: 50 ad creatives (10 per format × 5 formats)
- Ongoing: 20 new creatives/week to combat fatigue
- AI workflow: brief → ChatGPT copy (5 variants) → Midjourney visuals → Runway video → assemble
- A/B test cycle: 2-week sprints, statistical significance required before killing

## PHASE 5: CONVERSION & RETENTION (Week 3-ongoing)

### 5.1 Email Sequences
- **Post-scan nurture** (free users):
  - Day 0: Scan results + "here's what we found" (alarming tone)
  - Day 1: "3 things you should fix immediately"
  - Day 3: Case study — "How [Name] improved their visibility score from 34 to 87"
  - Day 7: Limited-time offer — first month $19 instead of $29
  - Day 14: "Your score hasn't changed. Here's what that's costing you."
  - Day 30: "Industry report: 67% of hiring managers check digital footprints"
- **Paid user onboarding**:
  - Day 0: Welcome + how to use the full dashboard
  - Day 3: "Quick wins — fix these 3 things today"
  - Day 7: Progress check + new insights unlocked
  - Day 14: Cross-sell — "Ready for the LinkedIn Optimization module?"
  - Monthly: Scan update — "Your digital footprint changed. Here's what's new."

### 5.2 Retention Mechanics
- Monthly automated re-scan with "change detected" alerts (creates ongoing value)
- Annual plan at 40% discount (lock-in)
- Cancellation flow: show what they'll lose → offer pause → offer downgrade → exit survey
- Free tier keeps them in ecosystem even if they cancel paid

### 5.3 Cross-Sell Expansion
- Digital Footprint Scan (entry point) → LinkedIn Profile Optimizer → Resume AI → Interview Prep Agent → Career Strategy Session
- Shared user profile data = zero friction to expand
- Bundle pricing for multiple tools
