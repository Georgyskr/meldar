## Key Decisions Made

1. **No fake social proof** — fake "As seen in" logos, AI testimonials, fake reviews are illegal in EU (UCPD, AI Act) and a brand contradiction for a digital footprint product
2. **Persona pages: 5-10 character brands, not 100+ fake people** — content brands like "The Hiring Insider" instead of "Dr. Sarah Chen, MD"
3. **Validate before scaling** — 2-channel validation sprint (Meta + Google) before expanding to 6 channels
4. **Product-led growth first** — instant shareable scan, referral from Day 1, free tools as SEO magnets
5. **SEO/AEO agency as first productized app** — 16 modular agents, API brokerage revenue model, EUR 19-69/mo
6. **Enterprise deferred to Week 16-20** — B2C proof points must come first
