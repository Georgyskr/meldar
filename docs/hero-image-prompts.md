# Hero Image Prompts for Leonardo AI

Brand colors: deep mauve #623153, warm peach #FFB876, cream surface #faf9f6
Vibe: Editorial warmth meets tech precision. Like Stripe's hero but warmer, more human.
Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 21:9 (wide hero banner)

---

## Option 1: Data Streams as Silk

Abstract flowing silk ribbons in deep mauve and warm peach tones, intertwining against a dark background, suggesting data streams becoming tangible and beautiful. Soft volumetric lighting from above, subtle glass-like refractions where ribbons cross. Shallow depth of field with bokeh particles floating in the air. Shot from slightly below eye level. Clean, editorial, premium feel. Color palette: deep purple-mauve to warm peach gradient. Ultra-detailed 3D render quality, cinematic lighting, 16:9 aspect ratio.

## Option 2: Phone Screen Dissolving into Light

Close-up of a smartphone screen from a low angle, the screen's light dissolving upward into warm golden-peach particles and geometric fragments floating in dark space. The phone itself is minimal, dark, barely visible — the focus is on the data escaping and transforming. Mauve and amber light rays. Tilt-shift lens effect. Dark moody background transitioning to warm light. Photorealistic 3D render, cinematic, 16:9.

## Option 3: Abstract Topography

Aerial view of an abstract topographic landscape made of layered paper or fabric in mauve, peach, and cream tones. Valleys represent time spent, peaks represent productivity. Soft directional lighting casting long shadows. Minimal, architectural, like a physical data visualization you could touch. Clean white edges. Inspired by Olafur Eliasson installations. Top-down camera angle, deep focus, 16:9.

## Option 4: Crystal Prism Data

A geometric crystal prism floating in soft dark space, refracting light into warm peach and mauve spectral bands. Inside the crystal, barely visible: abstract data patterns, numbers, app icons dissolving. The refracted light creates organic, flowing caustic patterns on the surface below. Studio lighting, one key light from upper left. Macro photography feel, extreme detail on crystal facets. 16:9.

## Option 5: Morning Light Through Blinds

Warm morning sunlight streaming through venetian blinds onto a desk surface, creating parallel lines of light and shadow. In the light: a coffee cup, a phone face-down. The light beams contain subtle data visualization particles — barely visible numbers floating in the warm golden rays. Quiet, intimate, human moment. Shot at 35mm, shallow depth of field. Color graded warm peach and cream. Photorealistic, editorial lifestyle, 16:9.

## Option 6: Neural Network Garden

An abstract garden where organic plant forms grow in deep mauve and peach colors, but the stems and branches form subtle circuit-like patterns. Bioluminescent nodes glow warmly at branch intersections. Soft fog at the base. Dark teal-black background. The feeling is alive, growing, organic technology. Macro lens perspective, selective focus on foreground cluster. 3D render, cinematic lighting, 16:9.

## Option 7: Time as Water

Abstract overhead shot of liquid in deep mauve color being poured into a pool of warm peach-golden liquid. The two colors meeting create intricate marbling patterns suggesting data merging. Tiny clock-like particles visible in the liquid surface. Clean white marble surface underneath. Flat lay photography style, studio lighting, symmetrical composition. Ultra clean, luxury brand aesthetic. 16:9.

## Option 8: Stacked Glass Panels

Layered semi-transparent glass panels at varying angles, each containing subtle data visualizations (graphs, timelines, app usage bars) in mauve ink on frosted glass. Light passes through the layers creating depth and overlap. The furthest panel is the most abstract, the closest is the most detailed. Warm peach light source from behind the stack. Architectural photography feel, deep focus, 16:9.

## Option 9: Hand Reaching Through Data

A human hand reaching upward through a cascade of floating abstract geometric shapes, data fragments, and soft light particles in mauve and peach. The hand is real and warm-toned, the data is ethereal and geometric. Contrast between human and digital. Dark background, dramatic rim lighting on the hand. Shallow depth of field, the hand in sharp focus, data shapes softly blurred. Cinematic, emotional, 16:9.

## Option 10: Origami Unfolding

A complex origami structure in cream paper, partially unfolded, revealing its inner geometry. The folds cast shadows in deep mauve tones. Warm peach light illuminates the unfolding edges. The metaphor: your data, unfolded and made visible. Single subject, clean dark background, studio product photography setup with soft overhead lighting. Shot on 100mm macro lens, medium depth of field. Minimal, Japanese-inspired aesthetic, 16:9.

## How to use in Leonardo AI

1. Copy any prompt above
2. Set model to Leonardo Phoenix or Kino XL
3. Aspect ratio: 16:9
4. Guidance scale: 7-9
5. Generate 4 variations, pick the best
6. Use image-to-image to refine if needed
7. Export at highest resolution

Place the final image at: `public/images/hero-bg.webp`
Then we'll wire it into HeroSection.tsx.
