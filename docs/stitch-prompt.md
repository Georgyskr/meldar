a# Meldar — Design Brief for Visual Identity

## The product

Meldar builds personal AI apps for non-technical people. You tell it what annoys you — "I spend 2 hours every Monday copying data from emails into a spreadsheet" — and it builds a personal app that fixes it. Permanently. Nobody else has your app. It's yours.

Three tiers:
- **Discover (free):** Watches which apps you switch between, spots repetitive patterns, suggests what to automate
- **Setup + Use (pay as you go):** Sets everything up for you. Click by click. Working AI in 30 minutes. Pay only for what you use.
- **We Build It (from $200):** Tell us what you need. AI builds your app while you watch progress live.

The name "Meldar" evokes melding — two things (you + AI) fusing into something new and personal.

## Target audience

**Primary:** Gen Z and Gen Alpha (18-28). Use ChatGPT daily. Know AI is powerful but every time they try to go deeper, they hit complexity walls. Frustrated, not ignorant. Want AI for THEIR specific life, not a generic chatbot.

**Secondary:** Corporate professionals who need hand-holding. But the brand is designed for the young audience first.

**Their emotional state:** "I've tried. I failed. The tools are too complex. I just want something that WORKS for ME."

## Brand values

- Confident but warm. Like a friend who's brilliant at this stuff.
- Direct. Short sentences. No fluff.
- Personal. This is YOUR app. Nobody else's.
- Non-technical. Zero jargon. Just human words.
- Young energy. Designed for people on TikTok and Instagram, not enterprise dashboards.

## What we need

### 1. Logo + Wordmark

**Name:** MELDAR

The core metaphor is "melding" — two things becoming one (you + AI). And the act of building something personal — like a human tapping into AI power to construct their own castle.

Explore ABSTRACT / VISUAL ICON directions (not a letter-based logo):
- A human figure reaching into / connecting with an abstract force, and something being built from that connection
- Two abstract shapes merging into one new form
- Building blocks assembling — a castle or structure taking shape from raw energy
- An overlap/intersection zone — where human meets AI, something new emerges
- A spark or ignition moment — the instant where intent becomes creation

Do NOT make the logo based on the letter M. We want an icon/symbol that works independently.

Requirements:
- Works in single color and in full color
- Readable as a favicon (16px) and as a hero element (200px+)
- Not a brain, robot, circuit board, or chatbot icon — those are cliches
- Should feel premium but approachable
- The icon should be abstract enough to scale with the brand but evocative enough to feel like "building something personal with AI"

### 2. Favicon + App icon
- The logo mark at all standard sizes (16, 32, 180, 512)
- Should pop on both dark and light backgrounds

### 3. Mascot / Character

We want a character or visual metaphor for "building your personal app."

**Core concept: A human tapping into AI to build their own castle**

This is the central visual story of the brand. A person reaches into (or connects with) an abstract AI force, and from that connection, a castle/tower/structure assembles itself — block by block. The castle represents their personal app. Every person's castle looks different because every person's app is unique.

Explore variations:
- Person placing their hand on a glowing surface, and above it a castle is assembling from floating pieces
- Person gesturing upward, blocks rising and snapping into a structure — playful, magical, empowering
- A progression sequence: person alone → person connecting with something → castle standing beside them
- The "building" should feel effortless for the person — they're not laboring, they're directing/commanding/creating with a touch

The character should feel:
- Curious and confident, NOT confused or struggling
- Young (our audience is 18-28)
- Gender-neutral or varied across uses
- Simple enough to work at small sizes but expressive enough to carry emotion

**Style:** Flat vector, friendly but not childish, "smart 22-year-old" aesthetic. Think ClickTheRoad's character quality but more geometric/modern.

### 4. Hero illustration

The landing page hero needs an illustration showing a 3-step story:

**Left:** Person at computer, slightly frustrated (the "before")
**Center:** Magic happening — blocks assembling, energy, motion (the "melding")
**Right:** Person happy/relaxed, with a small personalized app (castle/tower) beside them

Should read left-to-right as a narrative. Non-technical people should immediately get: frustration → magic → personal result.

## What to avoid

- Purple gradients on white (generic AI look)
- Robot or brain illustrations
- Enterprise/corporate dashboard aesthetics
- Stock photography
- AI-generated realistic/photorealistic images — everything should be illustrated, vector, or graphic. No fake photos, no "midjourney look", no uncanny valley faces.
- Blue as primary color (every AI company)
- Anything that looks like it was made for developers
- Visual complexity — our users are scared of complexity
- 3D renders or realistic textures — keep it flat, graphic, illustrated
